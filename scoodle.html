Scoodle for Secondary Teachers

Educational Platform

Role:

Research, UX Design, UI Design

Summary:

Scoodle is the online platform of Plantyn, a leading educational publisher in Belgium. They provide online materials and planning tools for teachers.

I was responsible for the total redesign and creation of the new classroom concept for secondary education. I also created a style guide for Scoodle and turned it into a responsive web application.
