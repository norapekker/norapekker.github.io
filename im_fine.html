I'm fine

Healthcare mobile application concept

Role:

Research, UX Design, UI Design

Summary:

I’m fine is a healthcare mobile application concept. It was created to help patients participating psychological therapies.
The main goal of the application is to maintain the contact between doctor  and patient in between two face to face sessions. It helps the doctors to monitor  the patient’s well being. It also provides transparency. The patient records are online available for herself as well.
