SETI Observe

Telescope Tracker

Role:

Research, UX Design

Summary:

SETI Observe is an institute dedicated to search for extraterrestrial life forms based on the radio signal search at the Allen Telescope Array.

The main goal of this site is to keep abreast of the ET signal search effort for the interested public . Visitors can follow along, see what they are observing, and be a part of the search.
I was working alone with a team of talented visual designers on this project. My role was to set up and conduct a research plan. And to deliver the UX Design.
